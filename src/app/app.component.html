Life of Adi

The story started on 28 May 1996. Even before I born my soul found it's partner. It's like a festival for Kotha's family. Anand rao garu and Rama Ratnam garu are on cloud nine 😍. Mr & Mrs Vinod Kumar was very cheerful 😊. Because a cute little boy is borned on that day. He is named as Kotha Venkata Aditya Prasanna 😜

This little boy is as cute as moon. He started looking each other, crying and making sounds. Then within few months he started crawling. Now it's time for "Annaprasana" 🍲😋. Soon after eating Pongal, the real task begins. It is nothing but picking an object which represents a prominent highlight in their future. Wait a minute... Guess what our hero picked 🤔. Okay I'll tell you, One hand with book 📖 and pen 🖊️ and other hand full of gold 🧈. Means now he will become a great scholar and earns handful of money 😜

This cute little boy became as notty as Krishna. He learned walking with help of parents and grand parents and started making small steps which is eye candy for them. Soon after he became 1, birthday bash started in the presence of family, relatives and friends 🎂🎁🎀✨. He is a prince in the family and everything he asks will be there infront of him 😶‍🌫️. But unfortunetly god decided to give compitition to him in the house, so he gave a brother to him 😝. He plays, fights, guides him and throws tantrums on him for his mistakes 😒.

Now our hero is in childhood stage. Whenever he gets a new toy he will be in full of joy and excitement 🤩. He will starts playing with it even it is mid night 🤾‍♂️. While playing suddenly a hidden scientists in him wakes up and wants to find how it was built 🤔. He dismantles everything but don't know how to fix it again and he will get scoldings from his father 😢. I'll tell you an example how mischievous our hero is... our boy and his brother brought a rented cycle to ride 🚴 and wants to ride it each for sometime, but our cunning boy misleads his brother saying to look the clock on hours hand instead of minutes hand ⏰. As his poor brother didn't know how to see time so he is looking when that hours hand will move, when his grand mother got to know all this thing she scolds our hero 😜.

It's time for our boy to go to school 🏫. He is very punctual and go to school on time. He studies well and scores good marks except maths. Maths is like a devil for him 👹. In summer holidays he goes to his grand mother's home and plays with neighbour kids, eats the favourite food cooked by grand ma, plays with the toys bought by mamayya and enjoys fully. But he can't stay out of Macherla for more days. He wants his grand father and grand mother to be besides him always. Even if they went to a pilgrimage tour he will get fever and gets them back, he loves them to that extent ❤️.

Everything went well so far, but unexpectedly for better education he has to leave Macherla and went to Vijayawada to study in Bhashyam 🏫. He felt so sad to leave his parents and grand parents and stay away from them. But he stood strong and continue to study out of town.

Now our little boy enters into teenage. He is studying intermediate in Sri Chaitanya Junior College. The college and the hostel resembles like a jail, yet our hero manages to find joy. He bunks the college, climbs the walls 🧗‍♂️ and chill out outside with friends. Weekend outings with family and holidays he will come to Macherla and spends time with family and friends. Everything is going very smoothly and happily but sadnely he came to know that his grand mother is suffering from cancer and is in advance stage. She had a good treatment, recovered but again effected with cancer and passed away. He felt so sad 😭. Out of all these he manages to score good marks in inter but fails to clear EAMCET. But he hides that matter from his father.

Now our hero started studying B.Tech in SRM University in Civil Engineering branch. Actually his dream is an Architect but due to some suggestions he opted Civil. Meanwhile his father came to know his EAMCET result and beat him badly 😢. But ya in university he got friends for life and he has best memories. suddenly he came to know that his grand father also passed away. He was schocked and felt so bad 😭. With the help of his friends he forgot all the pain and started leading normal. But due to one incident happened in bus he stopped talking with strangers 😜.

He likes bikes very much. So he bought bike secretly without the knowledge of his parents and started going to college like a hero(Arjun Reddy 😜) 🚴. He always wears branded dresses and gadgets and he is like a don for his batch🤠. Birthday parties, one night study, batch outings all came to an end. He completed his B.Tech and got graduated 👨‍🎓.

Some one said that life is a roller coaster. It is exactly true. Till now our hero don't know any struggles or difficulties. But everything will not be in our hands. He started searching for jobs, as he graduated as a Civil Engineer, he didn't get job in IT industry 👨‍💻 easily. He thought to join job in core side. So he joined as a Junior Civil Engineer 👷, then the real life struggles started. All the luxuries vanished away. He got sun stroke, body pains and many health issues as he worked in site irrespective of rain ⛈️ or shine 🌞. He won't have proper food 🍲 and bed 🛏️. Sometimes he used to eat the food supplied to workers and sleep in crane 🏗️. He don't know where his life is going, he is in dilemma. Finally he decided to resign this site work and want to join MBA.

He joined MBA in RICS Amity University, Noida 🧑‍🏫. He came to his normal life. He used to live in an gated community apartment with a cook. Because he tried to cook but he himself can't eat that 😜. Suddenly one day he got a call from EY company for a job oppurtunity as Pega Developer. He casually attends the company but out of surprise he got selected. He is very happy and without any second thought he dis-continued the MBA and came back to join job.

Our hero joined the company with lot of excitement, he got accommodation in a 5-star hotel during initial days. He chilled out gracefully and soon completed his training. Finally he joined in project, he did lot of hard work, worked late nights, learnt lot of things and got appreciation from on-site and off-shore team for being pro-active. He got hike and recognized in the ODC. He tried to shift the company with the knowledge he gained but EY doesn't want him to go, so they retained him and again gave good hike and promotion.

For his prayers and all the blessings from lord Siva he got enagaged with a beatiful, intelligent, soft and kind hearted girl 😜. The day she saw our hero, it's a magical moment for her. She lost her logical thinking and got speechless. They both have good compaitibility, there opinions got matched and built a great rapo. Within no time they found love between each other 💑.

27 January 2023, a memorable and great mile stone in their life's. They both tied knot and married 🔥. From then onwards, two persons became one, two souls combined into one. All the jokes, fightings, sarcasms every momemt is special to them. To the world he may be one person, but to her, he is the world. He is the love of her life 💖. They pinky promoised each other to be together and hold each hands through all the ups and downs and support each other to built the career they dreamed for 💞.
